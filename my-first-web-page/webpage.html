About Program

FreeCodeCamp is a non-profit organization that consists of an interactive learning web platform, an online community forum, chat rooms, online publications, and local organizations that intend to make learning software development accessible to anyone.

FreeCodeCamp offers a comprehensive curriculum on web development, including courses on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and more. Students learn by completing interactive challenges and building real-world projects. As they progress, they earn free verified certifications that demonstrate their skills to potential employers.
